What is airsoft?

Airsoft is a competitive shooting sport where players attempt to eliminate the opponent using firearm replicas that shoot 6mm plastic bbs. Airsoft originates from Japan in the early 1970s. Still to this day airsoft guns remain an invaluable training tool for civilians, law enforcement, and military. Airsoft is also a great, fun activity! Many people play airsoft just for the fun of it without regard for the training side, and that is perfectly alright as well! The goal of airsoft is to have safe fun.

Advantage Airsoft first opened its doors in February of 2019. Located in Menan, Idaho, this community driven hobby shop is the primary airsoft provider for all of Eastern Idaho.
